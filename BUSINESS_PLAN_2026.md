## Executive Summary

### Business Overview
**MarketScanner Pros** is an AI-powered market scanning and trading analytics platform designed for retail traders. The platform provides real-time market scanning, options flow analysis, crypto derivatives tracking, and AI-driven market insights.

### Mission Statement
To democratize institutional-grade trading tools for retail traders, making sophisticated market analysis accessible, affordable, and actionable.

### Key Highlights

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| **Product** | SaaS Trading Platform |
| **Target Market** | Active Retail Traders (US, AU, Global) |
| **Pricing** | $14.99 - $29.99/month |
| **Year 1 Revenue Target** | $25,000 - $50,000 |
| **Break-Even Point** | 18-36 users |
| **Year 1 Profit Target** | $15,000 - $30,000 |
| **3-Year Revenue Goal** | $500,000+ ARR |

### Unique Value Proposition
- **All-in-one platform**: Stocks, options, crypto in single dashboard
- **AI-powered insights**: GPT-driven market analysis and trade ideas
- **Affordable pricing**: 70-80% cheaper than competitors
- **Real-time data**: Institutional-grade data at retail prices
