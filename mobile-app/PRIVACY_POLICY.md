# Privacy Policy for Market Scanner Pro

**Last Updated: January 15, 2025**

## Overview

Market Scanner Pro ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Market Scanner Pro mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We Collect

The Market Scanner Pro mobile app is a WebView wrapper that displays our web application. As such:

- **We do not directly collect personal information** through the mobile app itself
- **All data collection** occurs through our web application at app.marketscannerpros.app
- **We do not access** device storage, camera, microphone, or location services
- **We do not use** tracking SDKs or third-party analytics in the mobile app

### Web Application Data

When using our web application (accessed through the mobile app or browser), we may collect:

- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent
- **Market Data Queries**: Symbols searched, alerts configured
- **Optional Account Data**: If you create an account (email, preferences)
- **Trade Journal Data**: Trades you manually enter (stored locally or in our database if you sync)

## How We Use Your Data

We use collected data to:
- Provide and maintain our service
- Improve user experience
- Send market alerts (if configured)
- Analyze usage patterns to improve features

## Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: Some preferences and data are stored on your device
- **Cloud Storage**: Account data and synced information stored securely on our servers
- **No Sale of Data**: We never sell your personal information to third parties

## Third-Party Services

Our web application may use:
- **Yahoo Finance API**: For market data (subject to their privacy policy)
- **Cloud Hosting**: Replit.com infrastructure (subject to their privacy policy)

## Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
