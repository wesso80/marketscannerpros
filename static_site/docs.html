MarketScanner Pro — User Guide

How scoring works, signals, alerts, and charts.

Quick Start

  1. Choose a watchlist (manual entry or import CSV).
  2. Click “Run Scanner” (Equities or Crypto).
  3. Open a symbol to see multi-timeframe confluence and indicators.
  4. (Optional) Set price alerts and export results for journaling.

Scoring & Weights

  • EMA Stack (30%)
  • SMA5↔EMA9/13 Trigger (10%)
  • RSI Regime (20%)
  • MACD Momentum (20%)
  • ATR Risk (10%)
  • Volume Context (7%)
  • Squeeze/Expansion (3%)

Confluence Stacks

TFs: 30m, 1h, 2h, 3h, 4h, 6h, 8h, 1D, Weekly. Stack tiers: ≥2 Watch · ≥3 Setup · ≥4 High-conviction · ≥5 Extreme.

Entries, Targets & Stops

Entry via SMA5 cross with EMA stack; ATR-based stops and targets.

Alerts

Auto Check cadence, price targets, and signal events.

Troubleshooting

  • No results? Loosen filters or expand watchlist.
  • Laggy? Fewer TFs or disable live refresh.
  • Mismatch? Check timezone/data source.
