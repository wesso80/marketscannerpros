Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-09-21

Market Scanner (“the App”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Account & Identifiers: The App itself does not require you to create an account. Apple may provide anonymous identifiers (e.g., for crash diagnostics).
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized usage and performance data (such as feature usage and crash logs) to improve stability and functionality.
  • Portfolio/Inputs: Any numbers you enter (e.g., tickers or portfolio inputs) are processed to render charts and analytics and are stored locally on your device unless you explicitly export or back up data.

How We Use Information

  • To operate core features like charting, backtesting, and portfolio analytics.
  • To troubleshoot, prevent errors, and improve performance.
  • To comply with legal obligations and App Store requirements.

Data Sharing & Transfers

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • Service providers (e.g., Apple, analytics or crash reporting vendors) may process limited data on our behalf under contractual safeguards.
  • If required by law, we may disclose information to authorities.

Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information. However, no system can be 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • You can reset, clear, or uninstall the App at any time to remove locally stored data.
  • You may opt out of Apple analytics and personalized ads in your iOS settings.

Children

The App is intended for users 16+ and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the updated version in this location and revise the “Last updated” date above.
